Audience

Our water rafting website is designed to cater primarily to adventure enthusiasts, thrill-seekers, and outdoor enthusiasts who are seeking exhilarating experiences in nature. Our target audience includes individuals aged 18-45, who are physically active, enjoy outdoor activities, and are looking for unique and exciting adventures to embark on.

Purpose

The purpose of establishing an online presence for my rafting business is multifaceted. Firstly, it's about accessibility and convenience for people to find information about our services, check availability, and even book trips online. Secondly, it's about visibility and marketing. An online presence allows us to reach a broader audience, including those who may not have heard of us through traditional means. We can showcase our unique offerings, share customer testimonials, and engage with our audience through content and interactions. Ultimately, the goal is to increase brand awareness, attract more customers, and grow the business.

Who will use the site and how will they use it?

Answer: Our target audience consists of adventure seekers who are likely to visit the site to gather information about our packages, check trip dates and availability, and ultimately make bookings for their desired adventure experience. They may also use the site to learn about safety measures, equipment requirements, and the overall experience we provide.
